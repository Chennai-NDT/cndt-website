Magnetic Particle Inspection Equipment

Magnetic particle inspection (MPI) testing equipment from Magnaflux is meant to be quick, dependable, and cost-effective. Magnaflux wet benches, magnetic yoke, and power pack are versatile and durable for industrial situations, and come in a variety of outputs, including AC, half-wave DC, and full-wave DC. Magnaflux MPI equipment can be customised to fit specific test parts and practically any part size.

Demag Unit

A demag unit, also known as a demagnetizer, is a device that removes magnetism from ferromagnetic surfaces and is used in magnetic particle inspection. Industrial degaussing is a complex task that necessitates the use of specialised equipment. High magnetic residues can cause harmful currents and voltages, but demagnetizing equipment can totally remove these magnetic fields.

Portable Magnaflux Machine

It may be used to illustrate a variety of MPI techniques, including Head Shot, Coil Shot, Threader bar method, and Cable wrap method. It is compact and low in weight.It is a mobile inspection equipment that may be used to check forgings, castings, heavy castings, heavy auto parts, insulated caps, weldments, pressure vessels, huge engine components, and maintenance work, among other things.
