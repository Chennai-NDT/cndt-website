Demag Unit

A demag unit, also known as a demagnetizer, is a device that removes magnetism from ferromagnetic surfaces and is used in magnetic particle inspection. Industrial degaussing is a complex task that necessitates the use of specialised equipment. High magnetic residues can cause harmful currents and voltages, but demagnetizing equipment can totally remove these magnetic fields.

Portable Machine
